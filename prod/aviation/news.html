DGS Wins Technology Award at the Mobile Enterprise 2012 Mobilizer Awards:

November 9, 2012 - Sarasota, FL - Mobile Enterprise magazine has awarded DGS' PAXTrack® dynamic wheelchair dispatch system the 2012 Mobilizer award in the Field Service Division. Along with business partners, GeoSpatial Technologies, Intermec and AT&T, DGS implemented the technology at the Memphis International Airport. Tom Farmakis, VP Marketing and Business Development, accepted the award on behalf of DGS. Tom commented, "This is a great honor and validation of our efforts to be recognized for what we have worked so hard to accomplish - that DGS is making a difference in improving the customer service experience through our technology."
